Organizations

The training division of the Finnish Mathematical Society organizes the competition

  • at Helsinki in cooperation with the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of the University of Helsinki and the Summamutikka Center.
  • at Oulu in cooperation with the Department of Mathematical Sciences of the University of Oulu and the OuLUMA Center.
  • at Turku in cooperation with the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of the University of Turku and Mathematics and Statistics of the Åbo Akademi University.
